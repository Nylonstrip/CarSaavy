What you get in every negotiation readiness report.

Negotiation stance & tone

Recommended posture based on vehicle age, segment, and mileage so you know whether to open curious, firm, or aggressive.

Price leverage zones

Clear guidance on where you likely have leverage vs. where the dealer does, so you don't fight the wrong battle.

Dealer pushback scripts

Specific language to respond when the dealer says things like “we're already below market” or “we don't discount used.”

Inspection & condition angles

Talking points to tie cosmetic and mechanical issues to real money at the desk, not just “can you fix this?”

Escalation or exit plan

When to involve a manager, when to pause, and when it's smarter to walk away and let the deal come back to you.

Segment & trim behavior

Guidance that respects how different segments behave (commuter sedan vs. truck vs. EV) so your expectations are realistic.
