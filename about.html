Our Mission

Buying a car shouldn't feel like you're walking into a trap. Most people dread the dealership because they don't know what questions to ask, what price is fair, or when to walk away. CarSaavy fixes that.

We turn raw vehicle data into actionable negotiation intelligence—specific pricing targets, dealer counterplay scripts, and walk-away triggers tailored to your situation.

What We Do

We analyze your vehicle's history, market positioning, condition risks, and buyer context to create custom reports that give you:

  • Specific pricing targets: Anchor points, target ranges, and walk-away thresholds
  • Dealer counterplay scripts: Exact responses to common pushback tactics
  • Inspection priorities: What to check and how to use findings for leverage
  • Strategic sequence: Step-by-step negotiation flow from first contact to closing

Why It Works

Most car buying advice is generic—"do your research," "know your budget," "be willing to walk away." That's not enough when you're sitting across from someone who negotiates for a living.

CarSaavy gives you vehicle-specific leverage and tactical scripts you can use in real-time. It's the difference between winging it and walking in with a battle plan.

Our Values

Honesty over hype. We won't promise you'll save $10k on every deal. We will give you clear, honest guidance so you never feel taken advantage of on price or condition.

Clarity over complexity. Our reports are designed to be readable and actionable, not buried in jargon or overwhelming data dumps.

Empowerment over dependence. We're not here to hold your hand through every deal forever. We're here to teach you how to negotiate confidently so you can do it yourself.
